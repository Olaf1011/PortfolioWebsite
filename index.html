About Me

I am a graduate from Sheffield Hallam. Where I did Msc Computer for Games. Here I studied a multitude of subjects. Including C++, C#, PlayStation 4, Unity, Vulkan API, DX12, DX11. I always love a challenge, and take one on when ever I can. Some of those challenges you will find on this site.

I'm a Dutch born and decided to do a full time study in England. Having now finished my degree I am happy I took on this challenge. As I learned a lot about pure C++ programming and graphics API's. I learned how to optimise code which I still hold dear and enjoy in my spare time.

Projects

Porting Zool to Vulkan

For our third year project we had the awesome opportunity to poort Zool Redimensioned to Vulkan. We go given the DX11 base and were left to our on accord to work together as a group and work together with Sumo Digital Acadamy to get Zool Redimensioned to run on Vulkan. We started from nothing as SHU (Sheffield Hallam University) does not teach Vulkan out of the box. We started with creating a renderer that renders a traingle to Zool Redimensioned running on Vulkan. We had to overcome many obstacles together and work as a team to finish this task with in the allotted 12 week period

Project Magma

Project Magma is my own renderer/framework using the Vulkan API. I created it after working on the Zool project and used my knownledge to create an improved version which I could reuse for other projects. I also created it for use in my dissertation, where I used to to create multiple different particle effects either running on the CPU or using the compute shader to run it purely on the GPU.
The source code is currently private as it is still being graded for its use in my dissertation

Labyrinthia: Lost Realms

Labyrinthia: Lost Realms is my masters final year group project. I was taken on by the Labyrinth Games team as they were taking their last years group project and improving on it. I took on the majority of the rendering part of the game. I redid the rendering layer allowing us to abstract the rendering from the framework. Making it easy for us to port the game to other platforms.
I also created the lighting for the game. Including normal mapping and other shader related parts of the game Including the damage effects, and some of the sounds for the game
